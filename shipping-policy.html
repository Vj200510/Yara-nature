Shipping Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Free shipping on orders above ₹499

1. Shipping Coverage

We deliver pan India — to all states and union territories across the country. Currently, we do not offer international shipping, but we're working on it!

2. Shipping Charges

Enjoy FREE shipping on all orders above ₹499. For orders below ₹499, a nominal shipping fee of ₹49 applies and will be displayed at checkout before payment.

Cash on Delivery (COD) orders have an additional COD handling charge of ₹49, regardless of order value.

3. Delivery Timeline

Here's what to expect after you place your order:

  • Order processing: 1–2 business days (Mon–Sat)
  • Metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, etc.): 2–4 business days after dispatch
  • Tier 2 & Tier 3 cities: 4–6 business days after dispatch
  • Remote areas: 5–8 business days after dispatch

Business days are Monday to Saturday, excluding public holidays.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email and SMS notification with your tracking number. You can also track your order anytime on our Track Order page using your Order ID.

5. Address Requirements

Please ensure your delivery address is complete and accurate, including:

  • House/Flat/Door number
  • Street name and area
  • City, State, and correct PIN code
  • A working phone number for delivery coordination

We do not deliver to P.O. Box addresses. Incorrect addresses may lead to delivery delays or failed deliveries.

6. Delivery Delays & Exceptions

Delivery timelines may occasionally be affected by:

  • Public holidays and festivals
  • Natural calamities or extreme weather
  • High order volume during sales
  • Courier partner delays in certain pin codes

We will proactively notify you of any significant delays via email or WhatsApp.

7. Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery fails due to an incorrect address or recipient unavailability, our courier partner will attempt 2 redeliveries. After that, the package is returned to us. You will be contacted to re-arrange delivery; reshipping charges may apply.

8. Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned to us as undeliverable, we will contact you to verify the address and reship at no additional cost, provided it was our or the courier's error. If the error was due to incorrect address information provided by you, reshipping charges will apply.

9. Contact Us

For any shipping queries, email support@yaranature.com or WhatsApp us at +91 99999 99999. Please include your Order ID for faster resolution.
