🌿 Usage & Application
How do I apply Yara Nature Hair Oil?

Spray or apply a few drops directly onto your scalp. Using your fingertips, massage in gentle circular motions for 5–10 minutes to improve absorption and blood circulation. Leave on for at least 30–60 minutes (or overnight for best results), then wash with a mild shampoo.

How often should I use it?

For best results, use 2–3 times per week. With consistent use, you'll notice visible improvement in hair fall reduction within 2–4 weeks.

Can I leave it on overnight?

Yes! Overnight application is actually recommended for deeper nourishment. Simply apply before bed and wash in the morning for maximum results.

Can I use it on wet hair?

It's best applied on dry or slightly damp hair for better scalp penetration. Avoid applying on soaking wet hair as it may dilute the oil's effectiveness.
