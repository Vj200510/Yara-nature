Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · 30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

1. Our 30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

At Yara Nature, your satisfaction is our highest priority. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase for any reason, we offer a hassle-free 30-day return and refund policy from the date of delivery. No questions asked.

2. Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, the product must meet the following conditions:

  • Purchased directly from our website (yaranature.com)
  • Return request made within 30 days of delivery
  • Product is in original or minimally used condition
  • Original packaging is intact (outer box, seal, etc.)

Products purchased from third-party marketplaces are subject to that platform's return policy, not ours.

3. How to Initiate a Return

To start a return or refund, contact us through any of these channels:

  • 📧 Email: support@yaranature.com
  • 💬 WhatsApp: +91 99999 99999 — send your Order ID and reason
  • 📞 Phone: +91 99999 99999 (Mon–Sat, 9AM–7PM)

Our team will respond within 24 hours and guide you through the return process, including arranging a free pickup if required.

4. Refund Processing

Once your returned product is received and inspected, we will notify you of approval. Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method:

  • Online payments (UPI/Cards/Net Banking): 5–7 business days to original account
  • Cash on Delivery orders: Refunded via bank transfer or UPI within 7 business days

5. Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with clear photos of the issue. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund — your choice — at absolutely no cost to you.

6. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for returns:

  • Gift cards and promotional items
  • Products that have been significantly used, tampered with, or damaged by the customer
  • Products returned after 30 days of delivery

7. Exchange Policy

We currently offer returns and refunds. If you wish to exchange for a different product, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order.

8. Contact Us

For any refund or return queries, email us at support@yaranature.com or WhatsApp us. We're here to help every step of the way.
