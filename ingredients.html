Power of 5 Natural Ingredients

Every drop is packed with nature's most powerful hair-healing herbs

🌾

Fenugreek (Methi)

Rich in protein and nicotinic acid, fenugreek seeds are one of Ayurveda's most celebrated remedies for hair fall. They strengthen hair roots, prevent premature thinning, and deeply nourish the scalp to encourage healthy regrowth. The natural lecithin in fenugreek also conditions hair, adding softness and shine.

🌶️

Clove (Laung)

Clove is a powerhouse of eugenol — a natural antifungal and antibacterial compound. It effectively fights dandruff-causing fungi, soothes scalp irritation, and improves blood circulation to the hair follicles. Better circulation means better nutrient delivery, which directly supports faster and thicker hair growth.

🌿

Rosemary

Clinical studies have shown rosemary oil to be as effective as minoxidil for stimulating hair growth — without the side effects. Rosemary activates dormant follicles, prevents premature greying, and improves scalp health. Its anti-inflammatory properties also help reduce scalp sensitivity and redness.

💊

Vitamin E

A potent antioxidant, Vitamin E protects hair follicles from oxidative stress caused by pollution and free radicals — one of the leading causes of hair thinning. It deeply nourishes the scalp, improves moisture retention, and leaves hair visibly smoother and more elastic, reducing breakage.

🫙

Natural Base Oils

A carefully balanced blend of lightweight natural oils forms the base of Yara Nature oil. These oils penetrate the hair shaft to condition from within, seal moisture, add a healthy natural shine, and create a protective barrier against heat and environmental damage — without leaving hair greasy or heavy.
